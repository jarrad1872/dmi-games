## DMI Integration Points

### Natural Product Placement
- Core drills for structural weak points
- Diamond blades for precision cuts
- Demo hammer for controlled demolition

### Tool Drop Triggers
- After first successful demolition
- On tool unlock
- When achieving high efficiency rating

### Upgrade System
- Basic tools → DMI Pro Line → DMI Expert Kit

---

## Feel Targets

### Visual
- Satisfying crumble physics
- Dust and debris particles
- Slow-motion impact moments
- Structural stress visualization

### Audio
- Impactful destruction sounds
- Tool operation sounds
- Building groans and creaks

### Haptic
- Strong vibration on impact
- Rumble during collapse
