# RHYTHM CUT - Storyboard

## Reference Game
**Name**: Beat Saber
**Platform**: VR (adapted for mobile)
**Why This Reference**: Perfect rhythm-action feel, satisfying slicing, music integration

---

## Core Loop

### 1. Entry Point
**Frame**: frame_001.png
**Description**: Song selection with difficulty
**Key Elements**:
- Song list
- Difficulty selector
- High score display

### 2. Main Gameplay
**Frame**: frame_002.png - frame_005.png
**Description**: Slice incoming notes to the beat
**Key Elements**:
- Notes approaching on lanes
- Directional indicators
- Blade trail effects
- Combo counter

### 3. Reward/Feedback
**Frame**: frame_006.png
**Description**: Song completion score
**Key Elements**:
- Final score
- Accuracy percentage
- Grade (S/A/B/C)
- New high score celebration

### 4. Progression
**Frame**: frame_007.png
**Description**: Unlock new songs and blades
**Key Elements**:
- Song unlock progress
- Blade collection
- Achievement badges

---

## DMI Integration Points

### Natural Product Placement
- DMI blades as slicing tools
- Blade skins match real products
- "Diamond Edge" blade for perfects

### Tool Drop Triggers
- After first S-rank
- On blade unlock
- After 100-combo streak

### Upgrade System
- Standard Blade → Segmented → Turbo → Diamond Elite

---

## Feel Targets

### Visual
- Clean note approach
- Satisfying slice trails
- Perfect hit explosions
- Miss feedback (not punishing)

### Audio
- Slice sounds synced to beat
- Music is central
- Combo sounds escalate

### Haptic
- Light tap on hit
- Strong buzz on perfect
