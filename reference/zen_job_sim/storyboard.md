# ZEN JOB SIM - Storyboard

## Reference Game
**Name**: PowerWash Simulator
**Platform**: PC/Console
**Why This Reference**: Extremely satisfying cleaning, zen-like flow, clear progression

### 1. Entry Point
**Frame**: frame_001.png
**Description**: Dirty job site overview
**Key Elements**:
- Before state clearly visible
- Job brief/objective
- Tool selection

### 2. Main Gameplay
**Frame**: frame_002.png - frame_005.png
**Description**: Cleaning/restoring job site areas
**Key Elements**:
- Dirt/grime visibly removing
- Percentage complete indicator
- Tool feedback effects

### 3. Reward/Feedback
**Frame**: frame_006.png
**Description**: Area completion celebration
**Key Elements**:
- Before/after comparison

**Frame**: frame_007.png

**Key Elements**:
- Tool upgrades

---

## DMI Integration Points

### Natural Product Placement
- DMI pressure washers and accessories
- Cleaning attachments as upgrades
- Real product specs in descriptions

### Tool Drop Triggers
- After first job completion
- On tool upgrade
- When discovering hard-to-reach areas

### Upgrade System
- Basic washer → DMI Pro → DMI Commercial Grade

---

## Feel Targets

### Visual
- Satisfying dirt removal
- Clean revealed surface
- Water/spray effects
- Progressive disclosure

### Audio
- Water spray sounds
- Satisfying "clean" ping
- Ambient job site sounds

### Haptic
- Light vibration during spray
- Satisfying pulse on area complete
